Variables You Can Control

Every element of the guest room environment can be optimized for restorative sleep.

18-20°C

Temperature Optimization

Core body temperature must drop 1-2°C for sleep onset. Thermal-regulating systems maintain the optimal zone throughout the night.

Okamoto-Mizuno & Mizuno, J Physiol Anthropol, 2012

0 lux

Complete Darkness

Light above 5 lux suppresses melatonin by 50%. Complete blackout protocols are essential for hormone optimization.

Phillips et al., PNAS, 2019

< 40 dB

Acoustic Control

Ambient noise causes micro-arousals that fragment sleep architecture. Pink noise can increase deep sleep by 25%.

Papalambros et al., Front Hum Neurosci, 2017

Warm-spectrum evening light preserves melatonin production. Dawn simulation optimizes the cortisol awakening response.

Nagare et al., Physiol Rep, 2017
