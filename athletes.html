The Science

Why Sleep Drives Performance

Understanding the mechanisms helps you appreciate why sleep optimization is as important as any other aspect of your training.

HGH

Growth Hormone

70% of daily HGH is secreted during deep sleep. This is essential for muscle protein synthesis, tissue repair, and adaptation to training loads.

REM

Motor Consolidation

During REM sleep, your brain replays and strengthens the motor patterns you practiced. This is how techniques become automatic.

HRV

Autonomic Recovery

Heart rate variability reflects nervous system recovery. Sleep quality directly predicts next-day readiness and performance capacity.

90m

Sleep Cycles

Understanding your 90-minute ultradian rhythms enables strategic scheduling of training, competition, and recovery.

Jet Lag

Travel Optimization

Strategic light exposure and timing protocols can compress multi-day jet lag adaptation into hours.

Naps

Strategic Napping

Properly timed naps can enhance afternoon performance, support recovery, and extend effective training capacity.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How quickly will I see results?

Many athletes notice improvements in perceived recovery and energy within the first 1-2 weeks of implementing protocols. Measurable performance gains typically emerge over 4-8 weeks as sleep optimization compounds.

I already sleep 8+ hours. Can you still help?

Absolutely. Sleep quality matters as much as quantity. Many athletes sleep adequate hours but miss critical deep sleep or REM phases. We optimize the architecture of your sleep, not just the duration.

How do you work with my existing performance team?

We integrate seamlessly with coaches, trainers, and sports scientists. Sleep data informs training load decisions, and our protocols complement existing periodization and recovery strategies.

What about competition schedules that disrupt sleep?

This is exactly what we plan for. We design protocols around your competition calendar, travel schedule, and the realities of elite sport—not idealized laboratory conditions.

Do you work with specific sports?

We work with athletes across all sports. While specific demands vary, the foundational science of sleep optimization applies universally. Protocols are customized to your sport's unique requirements.
