## Phase 3: Outreach Execution (Week 3-6)

**Goal:** Systematic outreach with tracking and follow-up

### Weekly Rhythm:
- **Monday:** Send 10-15 personalized outreach emails (mix of all 3 verticals)
- **Wednesday:** Follow up on emails from previous week (no response after 5 days)
- **Friday:** Update tracker, review responses, schedule any calls

### Email Sequence:
1. **Initial email** - Personalized intro, 1-2 relevant stats, soft CTA
2. **Follow-up 1** (Day 5) - "Wanted to make sure this didn't get buried..."
3. **Follow-up 2** (Day 12) - Share a relevant article/resource, add value
4. **Final follow-up** (Day 20) - "I'll assume timing isn't right, but here if useful..."

### Response Handling:
- **Interested:** Schedule 30-min discovery call with Alen
- **Not now:** Add to "nurture" list for quarterly check-in
- **No response after sequence:** Archive, retry in 6 months
